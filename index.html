04
How we measure 'working'

We'll watch the things that actually tell us it's working.

Your goals are awareness and real connection, so instead of follower counts and likes, we pay attention to whether the right people, the parents you want to reach, are finding you, and whether what they find is worth their time.

Awareness

Reach & non-follower reach

The clearest sign awareness is growing: whether people beyond your existing circle are seeing your work, not just the ones already following.

Awareness

Profile visits

The step between seeing a post and deciding to follow. It shows when curiosity is turning into genuine interest.

Audience fit

Geography & age

Since you want to reach a particular region and age group, we'll watch whether our content is actually getting to the right audience.

Connection

Saves

People keep what they want to return to. For teaching content, saves are a real signal it's useful, not just scrollable.

Connection

Comments & DMs

Actual conversations. We watch these most closely, because connection is the point, not applause.
